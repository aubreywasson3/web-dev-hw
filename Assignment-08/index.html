"Champagne Cupcakes"

Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sugar

- 1 3/4 Cups of Flour

- 1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter, Softened

- 2 Large eggs

- 1 tsp. of Vanilla Extract

- 1/4 tsp of baking soda, baking powder, and salt

- 1/2 cup of pink champagne


Making the Frosting

- 1 1/2 cup of Unsalted Butter

- 5 cups of powdered sugar

- 1 tsp. clear Vanilla Extract and almond Extract

- 3 to 4 drops of red food coloring


Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line cupcake tin with cupcake liners. In a large bowl, cream together sugar and butter until fluffy. Add eggs, vanilla and almond extracts and mix well.

In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.

In a THIRD bowl whisk together champagne and red food coloring.

Using and electric mixer, slowly add champagne mixture and flour mixture in to sugar and eggs. Alternate between the flower and the champagne as you are adding it to the butter and sugar mixture and mix slowly so your batter won't get too thick.

Bake 10 â€“ 15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Place on a cooling rack for 10 minutes. Pop cupcakes out of the pan and let them finish cooling on the rack for another 10 minutes.
