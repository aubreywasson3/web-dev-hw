The web is different than the internet because the internet is made up of a global network of networks and the web is a collection of information that is accessed through or via the internet. 
The internet is made up of a large number of independently operated networks. It is distributed by individual operators. It is made up of a massive network of specialized computers also called routers. Each router's job is to know how to move packets along from their source to their destination.
